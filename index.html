Changelog

v061726e · Lofi Livestream (June 17, 2026)
  • Background music is now the Lofi Girl livestream from YouTube instead of the in-engine procedural chords. The hidden YouTube player loads the stream on your first interaction (clicking Play) and the existing Music volume slider in Settings controls the livestream's volume directly
  • Fallback: if the YouTube embed fails to load (network blockers, autoplay restrictions, stream temporarily down), the game automatically falls back to the old procedural music so you're never left in silence
  • A small "now playing" indicator was added under the Music volume slider in Settings so you can see at a glance whether the livestream is active or the fallback is running
v061726d · Hot Reload (June 17, 2026)
  • Cache-busting: the game now always loads the latest deployed version. On every page load it fetches a tiny version.txt file (cache-busted with a timestamp) and compares it to the version baked into the HTML — if they differ, it force-reloads to pull the fresh deploy. You should never again be stuck playing a stale cached copy after an update goes live
  • Added HTTP cache-control meta tags (no-cache, no-store, must-revalidate) so cooperating browsers revalidate the HTML on every visit instead of holding a stale copy
v061726c · Roster Expansion (June 17, 2026)
  • Six new enemy types: Bomber (explodes on contact or death), Splitter (spawns mini-grunts when killed), Shielder (front shield blocks 80% of frontal damage — flank it), Summoner (spawns basic grunts every few seconds), Phantom (phases through walls), and Leech (heals itself when it damages you)
  • Small aim-assist: shots that just barely miss an enemy still clip it, so bullets that visually pass through the body silhouette reliably register
  • Changelog Back button moved to the top of the panel — you no longer have to scroll past every old entry to close it
  • Previous-version entries all dimmed to a uniform 60% opacity
v061726b · Aim Arc (June 17, 2026)
  • Player bullets now actually aim at the enemy model in 3D — they climb to hit enemies on top of walls and drop to hit grounded ones, instead of sliding flat along the ground and passing over/under them
  • Seeker missiles track the target's full 3D position and pitch up/down to follow it
v061726a · Recruiter's Refit (June 17, 2026)
  • The class-shop NPC in the lobby is now a full body that turns to face you and idles naturally — and you have to press E (or tap SHOP on mobile) to talk to it instead of the menu popping open on approach
  • Class prices rebalanced upward across the board so buying one feels like a real commitment
v061626l · Recruitment Drive (June 16, 2026)
  • Class system: a new NPC in the lobby lets you buy classes with shards and pick one to play as (Gunner, Ranger, Brute, Mage, Sprinter)
  • New enemy types — Shooter, Dasher and Healer — each labeled with its type above its health bar
  • Every arena map is now a random size
  • New weapons: Seeker (homing missiles) and Pulse (shockwave around you)
  • New upgrades: Berserker, Lethality, Bulwark, Glass Cannon, Momentum, Siphon, Rifling
  • Your bunnyhop speed trail no longer shows on the first hop — only once you start chaining

Leave the arena?

Stepping through the portal ends this run. Your level and all upgrades are wiped, and the next trip generates a fresh map. Shards you've collected are kept.

Classes

Buy classes with shards, then select one to play as. Your choice applies at the start of every run.

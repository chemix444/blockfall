Changelog

v061726n · UX Pass (June 17, 2026)
  • Bigger default bullet size — the uncharged ball was too small to see clearly
  • Removed the exit portal from the center of the arena (the End Run button replaces it)
  • Renamed "LEAVE" / "Quit to lobby" to "End Run" across all UI
  • Fixed pointer re-locking when clicking the settings gear after Esc — now only re-locks when a menu actually closes
  • Removed hit-stop on enemy hits/kills (was making the game feel choppy)
  • Cumulative damage numbers: hits on the same enemy stack into one number instead of spawning separate floating text per hit. The number's color/style reflects the latest hit (normal/crit/special)
  • Replaced muzzle-flash particles with a trail behind each projectile — bullets now leave a colored streak as they fly
  • Color customization moved into its own "Colors" submenu within Settings to keep the main settings panel less overwhelming
  • Added color customization for: bullet color, enemy body tint, arena ground, arena props, portal glow, and damage flash
  • Arena weapon panel now shows only the weapons you've unlocked (with names), instead of all weapons — matches the upgrade list style
v061726m · Refinements (June 17, 2026)
  • Colorblind mode now applies a CSS filter overlay on the canvas instead of swapping your color settings — your custom colors stay intact, and the filter (protanopia/deuteranopia/tritanopia/high-contrast) is applied as a real-time visual layer on top
  • New Cosmetics NPC in the lobby (mirrors the class NPC) — walk up and press E to open the cosmetic shop. Removed the cosmetics button from Settings since it now has its own NPC
  • Class Mastery is now a tab inside the Classes menu instead of a separate modal — switch between "Classes" and "Mastery" tabs at the top of the class shop
  • Charge shot GUI: a charge meter appears at the bottom-center of the screen when you hold Shift (or the new CHARGE button on mobile), showing the charge level from 0 to 100%. On mobile, a new CHARGE button appears next to JUMP — hold it to charge
  • Homing missiles reworked: now fire out of your BACK, fly backward briefly, then turn around and home toward the enemy with prop avoidance. Also have 2x your attack range so they can reach far targets
v061726l · Endless Portal (June 17, 2026)
  • Endless mode is now a separate orange portal in the lobby (next to the purple arena portal) instead of a settings toggle. Step through it to enter a pure survival mode: enemies of all types spawn continuously, a boss appears every 3 minutes, and there's no wave counter — just survive as long as you can. The HUD shows "ENDLESS" in orange with a boss countdown timer
v061726k · Mega Update (June 17, 2026)
  • Persistent stats page: tracks total kills, runs completed, deaths, bosses killed, time played, shards, per-class best wave, and kills by enemy type
  • Save export/import: export your save as a base64 code string to move progress between browsers
  • Colorblind mode: 4 presets that swap damage-number colors to higher-contrast palettes
  • Keybind remapping: click any keybind button in Settings, then press the new key
  • Pause menu: Esc in the arena now opens a Pause menu with Resume/Settings/Restart/Quit options
  • Hit-stop: 30ms freeze on crits, 60ms on kills, 120ms on boss kills
  • Crit visual polish: bigger gold damage numbers, screen-edge flash, sharper SFX
  • Damage number improvements: "BLOCKED!" and "BREAK!" text for shield interactions
  • Charged shot: hold LeftShift to charge a big shot (up to 2.5x damage)
  • Status effects: Frost (slow), Poison (DoT), Shock (chain) — 3 new upgrades
  • Upgrade reroll: spend 5 shards for a fresh set of 3 upgrades
  • Enemy spawn telegraph: red ring on the ground 0.5s before each spawn
  • Death slow-mo: time scales to 20% for 0.6s before the death screen
  • Elite enemies: every 5th spawn gets a random modifier (faster/shielded/exploding), 2x HP, 3x shards
  • Turret enemy: stationary shooter, spawns wave 7+
  • Mini-bosses: waves 5, 15, 25... spawn scaled-down elite bosses
  • Boss phases: Phase 2 at 50% HP, Desperate at 20% HP
  • Destructible props: ~30% of arena props break when shot
  • Biome variety: 2 new biomes (Tundra = ice, Emberfield = lava). 6 total
  • Day/night cycle: lobby sky shifts over a 5-minute cycle
  • Weapon switcher: pick starting weapon from Settings
  • Endless mode: toggle to hide wave counter, continuous scaling
  • Cosmetic shop: 8 free color presets
  • Class mastery: spend shards for permanent per-class stat boosts (max 5 levels)
  • Tutorial tips: first-run hints in lobby and arena
  • Object pooling: projectile meshes reused, reducing GC pressure
  • Replay: re-roll the last run's map layout
v061726j · Clarity (June 17, 2026)
  • Settings panel now always opens scrolled to the top — previously it remembered your scroll position from the last time you had it open, which was annoying when you'd scrolled down to change a color and reopened later expecting to see the top
  • Piercing shots now break Shielder shields: a piercing projectile that hits a Shielder's front shatters the shield for 3 seconds (the shield mesh visibly disappears with a small particle burst), during which frontal hits connect normally. Gives piercing builds a real counter into Shielder comps without trivializing them
  • New "Screen shake" toggle in Settings (on by default). Disabling it mutes all camera-shake effects — useful for motion-sensitive players or anyone who finds the new juice from v061726h disorienting
v061726i · Hint Lift (June 17, 2026)
  • Moved the contextual hint banner ("Press E to leave the run", "Step into the portal to enter the arena", "Tap SHOP to open the class shop", etc) above the health bar so it's not obscured by it. Previously the hint sat at the same height as the HP bar and the two overlapped, making the hint hard to read at the worst moments
v061726h · Juice (June 17, 2026)
  • Screen shake: the camera now shakes on impact — small when you take damage, medium on boss attacks and boss spawn, big on Bomber explosions. Decays over ~0.4s so it punches without making you seasick
  • Muzzle flash: a small spark burst now puffs out of the player's chest every time a projectile fires, tinted to match the projectile's color. Pure juice — no gameplay effect, just feels better to shoot
  • Boss intro banner: when a boss spawns (every 10th wave) the screen darkens briefly and a big dramatic banner with the boss's name fades in over ~2s before fading out. Replaces the easy-to-miss toast notification
  • Run summary on death: the death screen now shows your wave reached, total kills this run, and time survived — so you can see at a glance how the run went before heading back to the lobby
v061726g · Action Poses (June 17, 2026)
  • Player jump animation: while airborne the avatar tucks its knees up and raises its arms — different pose on the way up (apex stretch) vs the way down (legs tucked for landing) — and smoothly blends back to idle/walk on touchdown
  • Enemy climbing animation: when a regular enemy scales a wall its legs alternate vertically (real climbing motion) and its arms reach up — instead of doing the walking swing while it climbs
  • Enemy dashing animation: while a Dasher (or boss) is mid-lunge, the body pitches forward, arms swing back, and legs extend backward — reads as a committed dive instead of just translating forward
v061726f · Idle Bob (June 17, 2026)
  • Player avatar now has an idle animation: subtle breathing (torso bob) plus a small arm sway and leg weight-shift while standing still, so you no longer look frozen mid-stride when you stop moving. Smoothly blends with the walk cycle when you start moving again
v061726e · Lofi Livestream (June 17, 2026)
  • Background music is now the Lofi Girl livestream from YouTube instead of the in-engine procedural chords. The hidden YouTube player loads the stream on your first interaction (clicking Play) and the existing Music volume slider in Settings controls the livestream's volume directly
  • Fallback: if the YouTube embed fails to load (network blockers, autoplay restrictions, stream temporarily down), the game automatically falls back to the old procedural music so you're never left in silence
  • A small "now playing" indicator was added under the Music volume slider in Settings so you can see at a glance whether the livestream is active or the fallback is running
v061726d · Hot Reload (June 17, 2026)
  • Cache-busting: the game now always loads the latest deployed version. On every page load it fetches a tiny version.txt file (cache-busted with a timestamp) and compares it to the version baked into the HTML — if they differ, it force-reloads to pull the fresh deploy. You should never again be stuck playing a stale cached copy after an update goes live
  • Added HTTP cache-control meta tags (no-cache, no-store, must-revalidate) so cooperating browsers revalidate the HTML on every visit instead of holding a stale copy
v061726c · Roster Expansion (June 17, 2026)
  • Six new enemy types: Bomber (explodes on contact or death), Splitter (spawns mini-grunts when killed), Shielder (front shield blocks 80% of frontal damage — flank it), Summoner (spawns basic grunts every few seconds), Phantom (phases through walls), and Leech (heals itself when it damages you)
  • Small aim-assist: shots that just barely miss an enemy still clip it, so bullets that visually pass through the body silhouette reliably register
  • Changelog Back button moved to the top of the panel — you no longer have to scroll past every old entry to close it
  • Previous-version entries all dimmed to a uniform 60% opacity
v061726b · Aim Arc (June 17, 2026)
  • Player bullets now actually aim at the enemy model in 3D — they climb to hit enemies on top of walls and drop to hit grounded ones, instead of sliding flat along the ground and passing over/under them
  • Seeker missiles track the target's full 3D position and pitch up/down to follow it
v061726a · Recruiter's Refit (June 17, 2026)
  • The class-shop NPC in the lobby is now a full body that turns to face you and idles naturally — and you have to press E (or tap SHOP on mobile) to talk to it instead of the menu popping open on approach
  • Class prices rebalanced upward across the board so buying one feels like a real commitment
v061626l · Recruitment Drive (June 16, 2026)
  • Class system: a new NPC in the lobby lets you buy classes with shards and pick one to play as (Gunner, Ranger, Brute, Mage, Sprinter)
  • New enemy types — Shooter, Dasher and Healer — each labeled with its type above its health bar
  • Every arena map is now a random size
  • New weapons: Seeker (homing missiles) and Pulse (shockwave around you)
  • New upgrades: Berserker, Lethality, Bulwark, Glass Cannon, Momentum, Siphon, Rifling
  • Your bunnyhop speed trail no longer shows on the first hop — only once you start chaining
